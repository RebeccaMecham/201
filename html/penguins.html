Penguins

Creatures of the Southern Hemisphere

There are 19 different types of penguins, found in a varity of contries and continents in the southern hemisphere. Though most commonly associated with Antartica, they originally came from Australia, New Zealand and some additional South Pacific islands around 22 million years ago. Fossil evidence has been found of human-size penguins that existed around 30 million years ago.
